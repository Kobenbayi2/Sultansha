Start with short sessions:

It is recommended to start with short sessions (for example, 10-15 minutes) and gradually increase their duration and frequency if there are no side effects.
Session duration:
Usually, an LED therapy session lasts from 10 to 30 minutes, but this may vary depending on the specific mask color and the problem being treated.
Do not overdo it:
Excessive use of the mask can negatively affect the skin, so it is important to follow the manufacturer's recommendations and not abuse it.

Eye protection:

When using the mask, be sure to protect your eyes from light using special glasses or place cotton pads on closed eyes.
Consult a doctor:
If you have had eye surgery in the last six months;
If you have any skin or eye diseases, or if you are taking any medications, consult a dermatologist before using the LED mask.
Average course:
The average course of LED therapy is 8-12 sessions every 2-3 days, then maintenance procedures can be performed 1-2 times a week.
